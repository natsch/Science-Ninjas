Real Science

Valence teaches kids REAL chemistry! Players draw cards that represent 9 elements from the periodic table of elements. To win the game, they need to combine these elements based on their valence number - the number of electrons they can give, or the number of electrons they need to make a full valence shell. Add the valence numbers up to zero and you've made a molecule! Make enough molecules and you win the game.

In addition, players' molecules can react with each other just like they do in nature! Neutralize a player's base with an acid, and it turns into a salt and a water!
