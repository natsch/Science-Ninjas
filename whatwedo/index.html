Real Science

While everything we teach is within the grasp of the average elementary student, we pride ourselves on teaching fundamental science. Our curriculum is developed by two PhDs and focuses on Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science, and Engineering. Further, our content aligns with science standards across 50 states, including the newly developed Next Generation Science Standards.

Why Comics?

Besides a history of engaging young minds, cartoons visualize scientific material that often gets very big, very small, or very abstract. Scientists use comics to explain their work all the time - they just call them models or diagrams.
