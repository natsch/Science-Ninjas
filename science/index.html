Add Science to Your Camp or After School Program!

Enhance your after school program or summer camp with our Science Ninja MISSIONS! Kids learn by doing with hands-on experiments and collaborative design competitions, while comics and animations teach key concepts and reveal what can’t be seen!

Single Events - 60-90 minutes

Rocket Ninjas - Find out how rockets work and build your own! Use chemical reactions and the laws of motion to create contained explosions that send your rocket skyward! Kids keep the rocket so they can do the experiment at home!

Beaker of Mystery - Create explosions of swirling colors and bubbles in a quest to identify six mystery materials! This class really lets kids explore and make their own discoveries - just like real scientists!

Journey to the Bottom of the Sky - Learn gravity and more importantly, how to defy it! If you've ever wondered how airplanes fly, this is the perfect class!

Nautical Ninjas - Use the science of buoyancy and design your fleet to carry the most candy! Get your creative juices flowing with this perfect mix of engineering and design.

Week-long Programs - Five 60-minute sessions

As Strong As Water - Build a device that re-creates the water cycle as you discover the secrets of nature’s most amazing substance!

In Between Atoms - Explore the atomic nature of everything by building chemical reactors and creating contained explosions!
