Characters

Eureka Fermi

11-Year Old Eureka Fermi has a PhD in Chemical Engineering and black belts in Judo, Jujitsu, and Taekwondo. Even in tense situations, the talented and brilliant Science Ninja keeps her cool. She's usually found chewing a brand of sugarless bubblegum she synthesized herself.

Carlos Einstein

Carlos Einstein has been genetically engineered to be faster, stronger, and more durable that any 10-year-old on the planet. While not a genius like his commanding officer Eureka, Carlos has a good spirit and a broad curiosity about the natural world. Carlos is just a cadet and not a full-ranked Science Ninja.
