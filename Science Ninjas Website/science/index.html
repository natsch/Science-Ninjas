Add Science to Your Camp or After School Program!

Enhance your after school program or summer camp with our Science Ninja MISSIONS! Kids learn by doing with hands-on experiments and collaborative design competitions, while comics and animations reinforce key concepts and reveal what can’t be seen! Learn Science the Ninja Way!

Single Events - 60-90 minutes

Rocket Ninjas - Find out how rockets work and build your own!

Beaker of Mystery - Create explosions of swirling colors and bubbles in a quest to identify six mystery materials!

Journey to the Bottom of the Sky - Learn gravity and more importantly, how to defy it!

Nautical Ninjas - Use the science of buoyancy and design your fleet to carry the most candy!

Week-long Programs - Five 60-minute sessions

As Strong As Water - Build a device that re-creates the water cycle as you discover the secrets of nature’s most amazing substance!

In Between Atoms - Explore the atomic nature of everything by building chemical reactors and creating contained explosions!
